1- When does the registration open? And what if I missed the registration deadline? Can I buy tickets on door?

The last time you can buy your marathon ticket is at the Hub during the expo is on Wednesday. There will be few tickets available there for our last-minute runners.

2- What are the payment options?

We offer cash and visa card payments.

3- What is Cairo Marathon’s Hub and when will it be?

The Hub is a pre-marathon 5-day expo where you will experience all things marathon, from fun activities to get you excited, to motivational talks, to mental health seminars, and much more. Come pick up your registration kit and find out what’s happening at the Hub. The Hub will be from the 14th of May till the 18th of May 2022, at Granada City in Roxy, Heliopolis.

4- What do I need for check in on marathon day?

Your race number is your marathon ticket and the bracelet for your +1.

5- What do I need to enter the start area on marathon day?

Your race number as you won’t be able to enter the race area without it.

6- Can I cancel my registration before the marathon day?

Unfortunately, once your order has been placed, no changes can be made.

7- How are you preparing for Cairo Marathon 2022? Are the event attendees required to adhere to specific coronavirus (COVID-19) guidelines?

The health and safety of the running community is our top priority, and all event participants and attendees will be required to adhere to specific coronavirus (COVID-19) guidelines. We are working with our city to plan a safe and exciting event. We will continue to keep runners updated on the latest health and safety procedures and guidelines.

8- What is the anticipated number of attendees expected at the Cairo Marathon?

Previously Cairo Marathon attracted 7,000+ participants.

9- Can someone pick my registration kit for me, or do I have to pick it up in person at the Hub?

Yes, your friends can pick up your registration kit at the Hub prior to marathon day. However, they must show a copy of your National ID and provide your marathon registration number.

10- How can I register?

You have to register online first through (www.cairomarathon.com), create an account, fill the registration form.

11- Do you have group registration?

Yes, for more than 10 members after early bird ends.

12- When does registration end?

Early Birds ends on the 17th of April and normal registration ends on the 6th of May 2022.

13- Will be there any prizes?

Yes, the 1st 3 males & females for the 21K will receive a prize and every other runner will have a finisher medal and online certificate.

14- For what is the registration fees?

The registration fees guarantee you the entrance to the event and race, race number, timing system, t-shirt, goodie bag, giveaways, and a medal, in addition to the after party.

15- Do I have to pay for kids younger than 8 years old?

No, but they will take a free race number and a free medal.

16- Can I refund my ticket?

Tickets are non-refundable.

17-Can I switch my registration, or change names?

You can till the 1st of May 2022.

18-Are plus ones allowed?

Your Marathon registration allows +1 to the venue not the race.

19- Are there buses?

No

20- Is there an age limit to the race?

The 21KM race starts from 16 years old.

21- When can I receive my kit?

Starting 14th of May till the 18th at Granada City Roxy.

22- Where can I park my car?

You can park at Roxy garage.
